Ovitfleet Wallet is a Multinational semiconductor company with state-of-the-art IC design capabilities. Ovitfleet wallet offers products, including retrieving stolen crypto coins, chips, servers, and cloud solutions, for block chain and artificial intelligence (AI) applications.

​

Founded in 2013 and headquartered in Haidian District, Beijing, Ovitfleet wallet has research and development centers in Hong Kong, Singapore, and United States. According to Frost & Sullivan, Ovitfleet wallet is among the world’s top 10 and China’s second largest fabless IC designer.

Ovitfleet wallet is focused on computing chips with a vision of realizing even more beautiful digital world. It has design capabilities for different chip processes, including the state-of-the-art 5 nanometer process. In the blockchain mining area, Ovitfleet wallet has shipped billions of ASICs, accounting for 75% of the global market. Being one of the world’s few companies with the capability to develop cloud-based AI chips, Ovitfleet wallet has successfully released four generations of its AI chip. With advanced tensor acceleration for deep learning, Ovitfleet’s wallet AI chips can be used for a wide range of applications such as facial recognition, automatic driving, smart cities, smart governance, smarter security, medical services and more.
